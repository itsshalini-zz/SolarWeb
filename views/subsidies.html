Subsidies in India

Megawatt Solutions is an active channel partner of MNRE, and our projects are eligible for all subsidies.

All following three subsidies are applicable on Dual Axis Megawatt Solutions Paraboloid Dish (M95, Aperture Area 95 m2)
  • Rs. 6000 per sq. mtr. from MNRE (30% of benchmark cost of Dish)
  • Rs. 4000 per sq. mtr. from UNDP (20% of benchmark cost of Dish)
  • 80% Accelerated Depreciation benefit in first year
Applicable subsidy on Dual Axis Megawatt Solutions Paraboloid Dish (M95, Aperture Area 95 m2) for Special Category States, eg. Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal, Uttrakhand, NE Region, islands, districts with international borders
  • Rs. 12000 per sq. mtr. from MNRE (60% of benchmark cost of Dish for non-profitable institutions)
  • Rs. 6000 per sq. mtr. from MNRE (30% of benchmark cost of Dish for profitable institutions)
  • Rs. 6000 per sq. mtr. from MNRE (30% of benchmark cost of Dish)
  • Rs. 4000 per sq. mtr. from UNDP (20% of benchmark cost of Dish)
  • 80% Accelerated Depreciation benefit in first year for profitable institutions
Note
  • Subsidy is indicative only, and subject to change.
  • Prior project sanction/approval from ministry is required beforehand for availing subsidies
